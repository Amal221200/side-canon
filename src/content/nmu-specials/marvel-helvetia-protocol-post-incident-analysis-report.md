## ZURICH INFRASTRUCTURE EVENT

**Subject: Quentin Beck**

**Classification Level: Declassified – Strategic Review**

### I. SUBJECT PROFILE

**Name:** Quentin Beck

**Former Position:** Director, Immersive Systems Division, Aurora Dynamics

**Specialization:** Volumetric simulation, perceptual architecture, real-time narrative control systems

Beck was a senior systems architect specializing in immersive perception overlays. His division focused on next-generation urban simulation infrastructure designed to influence environmental response behavior.

He is not a mystic.

He is a perception engineer.

### II. IDEOLOGICAL DISPUTE

Aurora Dynamics shifted strategic direction five years prior to the Zurich Event.

Aurora adopted:

- Predictive stabilization systems
- Decentralized swarm architecture
- Ethical AI constraint models
- Infrastructure resilience frameworks

Beck advocated:

- Immersive dominance systems
- Centralized perception control
- Behavioral guidance architecture
- Narrative-driven crisis management

Aurora leadership determined immersive dominance models were:

- Centralization-dependent
- Ethically volatile
- Vulnerable to authoritarian misuse
- Structurally unstable under cascading failure

Beck publicly disagreed.

He departed Aurora within six months of the division’s dissolution.

- Deprecated Aurora routing architecture
