**Classification: Restricted – Urban Defense Coordination**

## **I. ORGANIZATIONAL PROFILE**

Helvetia Protocol operates as a government-supported, semi-independent rapid response unit specializing in:

- Urban stability operations
- Counter-terror intervention
- High-tech crime suppression
- Infrastructure protection

They are not a publicity arm.

They are a containment unit.

Primary Doctrine:

Prevent escalation.

Stabilize rapidly.

Withdraw quietly.

---

## **II. CORE RESPONSE TEAM – ZURICH DIVISION**

Team size: 4 operational members.

Each assigned a defined tactical role within structured engagement parameters.

No redundancy.

No spectacle.

Precision-based deployment.

### 1️⃣ FIELD COMMANDER

**Codename:** Vector

**Operational Role:** Battlefield Orchestration

**Ability Profile:**

Kinetic energy absorption and controlled redistribution.

Vector can:

- Absorb physical impact
- Redirect stored force in calibrated bursts
- Reinforce defensive lines through momentum conversion

**Engagement Style:**

- Efficient
- Calculated
- Minimal verbalization
- Pattern-driven

Vector thrives in predictable combat systems.

Illusion-based warfare destabilizes his calculation models.

During the Zurich Event, false target mapping significantly reduced his early engagement accuracy.

He mirrors mechanical logic — structured, force-balanced.

### 2️⃣ BARRIER SPECIALIST

**Codename:** Aegis

**Operational Role:** Civilian Protection & Containment

**Ability Profile:**

Hard-light shielding and sector isolation constructs.

Aegis can:

- Deploy mobile barrier walls
- Encapsulate impact zones
- Partition active threat sectors
- Maintain evacuation corridors

**Engagement Style:**

- Defensive priority
- Structured containment
- Controlled escalation

She does not pursue targets.

She limits damage.

During the Zurich Event, containment fields successfully mitigated secondary structural collapse.

### 3️⃣ TECH OPERATIVE

**Codename:** Pulse

**Operational Role:** Signal Detection & Systems Analysis

**Ability Profile:**

Electromagnetic field mapping and drone-frequency tracking.

Pulse functions as:

- Tactical sensor grid
- Signal anomaly analyst
- Infrastructure relay specialist

During initial engagement, Pulse’s instruments registered biological null-readings.

However, projection-layer interference masked true drone signatures.

Projection density overwhelmed conventional scan architecture.

Instrument glitching was the first detectable structural inconsistency.

Pulse mirrors decentralized logic — but was initially disrupted by centralized immersive masking.

### 4️⃣ MOBILITY SPECIALIST

**Codename:** Zephyr

**Operational Role:** Vertical Engagement & Rapid Evacuation

**Ability Profile:**

Air-channel manipulation and enhanced aerial maneuvering.

Zephyr can:

- Redirect wind vectors
- Achieve sustained mid-air mobility
- Intercept airborne threats
- Conduct high-altitude civilian extraction

**Engagement Behavior:**

- Aggressive aerial pursuit
- Precision interception
- High-speed response

During the Zurich Event, Zephyr engaged projected dragon-class entities mid-air.

Initial attacks appeared effective.

Creatures reformed.

Engagement instability disrupted confidence metrics.

First psychological destabilization recorded within Helvetia team structure.

## **III. STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS – ZURICH EVENT**

Helvetia Protocol operates optimally in:

- Pattern-based combat
- Defined threat parameters
- Infrastructure-backed response

Mysterio’s projection architecture introduced:

- Perceptual distortion
- False feedback loops
- Non-linear threat regeneration
- Centralized manipulation layered over decentralized defense

This temporarily disrupted:

- Vector’s predictive modeling
- Pulse’s detection grid
- Zephyr’s threat confirmation
- Aegis’ containment calibration

Helvetia was not defeated.

They were destabilized.
